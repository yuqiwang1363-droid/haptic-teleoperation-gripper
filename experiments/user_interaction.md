# User Interaction Improvement

---

## Overview

During early testing, the interaction method between the user and the device was found to be unstable and unintuitive.

This led to a redesign of the interaction interface to improve usability and control.

---

## Problem Description

* The initial interaction relied on a pinch gesture using two fingers
* The grip was unstable during operation
* Users had difficulty maintaining consistent control
* Interaction felt unnatural, especially during continuous motion

This indicated that the original design lacked sufficient support for stable manipulation.

---

## Root Cause Analysis

The issue was primarily due to the lack of a stable interaction interface:

* No structural support for the user's hand
* The pinch-based interaction required continuous fine motor control
* Small disturbances led to large motion variations

---

## Design Modification

To improve usability, a vertical handle was introduced:

* A rigid vertical grip was added to the end-effector
* The handle allowed users to grasp the device more naturally
* Provided better support and control during operation

---

## Results

After introducing the handle:

* Interaction became significantly more stable
* Users could maintain better control over motion
* The overall user experience was improved

---

## Engineering Insight

This improvement highlights that:

> Human-machine interaction design is critical in haptic systems.

Providing a stable and ergonomic interface can greatly enhance system usability and performance.

# 人机交互优化

---

## 概述

在初期测试中发现，用户与设备之间的交互方式不稳定且不直观。

因此对交互方式进行了改进，以提升系统的可用性与控制稳定性。

---

## 问题描述

* 初始采用双指捏合方式进行操作
* 操作过程中稳定性较差
* 用户难以持续保持精确控制
* 在连续运动过程中交互不自然

说明原始设计缺乏稳定的操作支撑结构。

---

## 原因分析

问题主要在于交互方式本身：

* 缺乏手部支撑结构
* 捏合操作依赖精细控制
* 微小扰动会导致较大误差

---

## 改进方案

引入垂直握把（vertical handle）：

* 在末端增加刚性握把结构
* 允许用户以更自然的方式握持设备
* 提供稳定的操作支撑

---

## 改进效果

改进后：

* 操作稳定性显著提升
* 用户更容易控制设备运动
* 整体交互体验明显改善

---

## 工程启示

该改进表明：

> 人机交互设计在触觉系统中具有重要作用。

合理的结构设计可以显著提升系统的可用性与控制性能。
