# Gripper Design Iteration

---

## Overview

During early testing, the gripper design was found to limit the user's ability to perceive object size and perform stable manipulation.

This led to a redesign of the gripper geometry to improve tactile interaction and usability.

---

## Problem Description

* Users could not fully close their fingers when interacting with the gripper
* The distance between the two contact surfaces was too large
* Tactile perception of object size was poor
* Interaction felt unnatural and imprecise

This indicated that the initial design did not adequately consider human ergonomics.

---

## Root Cause Analysis

The issue was primarily caused by the geometry of the gripper:

* The front section of the gripper was too thick
* The contact region between the user's fingers and the gripper was too far apart
* The design prioritized structural simplicity over user interaction

As a result, users were unable to achieve natural pinch-like interaction.

---

## Design Modification

To address these issues, the gripper geometry was redesigned:

* The front section of the gripper was tapered (reduced thickness)
* The contact region was brought closer to allow better finger closure
* The shape was adjusted to improve ergonomic compatibility

---

## Results

After redesign:

* Users could comfortably close their fingers around the gripper
* Tactile perception of object size was significantly improved
* Interaction became more natural and controllable

---

## Engineering Insight

This iteration demonstrates that:

> Mechanical design must consider not only structural integrity, but also human interaction.

Even small geometric changes can significantly affect usability and perception.

# 夹爪结构优化

---

## 概述

在初期测试中发现，夹爪结构设计限制了用户对物体尺寸的感知能力，同时操作稳定性较差。

因此对夹爪结构进行了优化，以提升触觉交互效果与使用体验。

---

## 问题描述

* 用户无法完全闭合手指进行操作
* 两个接触面之间距离过大
* 对物体尺寸的感知能力较差
* 操作过程不自然，控制精度低

说明初始设计未充分考虑人机交互需求。

---

## 原因分析

问题主要来源于夹爪的几何结构：

* 前端结构过厚
* 手指接触区域间距较大
* 设计更偏向结构实现，而非操作体验

导致用户无法实现自然的“捏合”动作。

---

## 结构改进

针对该问题进行了如下优化：

* 对夹爪前端进行斜向减薄（tapered design）
* 缩小接触区域之间的距离
* 调整整体形状以提升人体工学适配性

---

## 改进效果

优化后：

* 用户可以自然闭合手指进行操作
* 对物体尺寸的感知明显提升
* 操作更加稳定、自然

---

## 工程启示

该改进表明：

> 机械设计不仅要满足结构需求，还需要考虑人机交互体验。

细微的结构变化也可能对系统性能产生显著影响。
